PHASE 01: INFRASTRUCTURE TAKEOVER & FBM LAUNCH

Zero-to-One Market Penetration (Amazon.sg)

Upon finalizing the partnership, our operational team engineered the brand's complete storefront architecture on Amazon Singapore. Launching a global FBM (Fulfillment by Merchant) model with absolutely zero baseline seller feedback is notoriously difficult in the highly competitive Asian market. However, by deploying precise listing optimization and aggressive penetration strategies, we successfully stimulated initial market traction and initiated sales velocity within the first 7 days of deployment.

PHASE 02: CONVERSION ENGINEERING

Listing Optimization & Strategic Loss-Leading

Following the initial launch, we executed a complete overhaul of the brand’s digital merchandising. Every ASIN was re-engineered to achieve a flawless 10/10 retail readiness score.

Simultaneously, we established a localized customer success team with a strategic "loss-leading" protocol: issuing unconditional full refunds and free replacements for unreturnable items. This short-term margin sacrifice aggressively scaled brand equity and secured a flawless storefront rating.

PHASE 03: REVENUE VELOCITY & MARGIN VALIDATION

Month-One Financial Trajectory: $23k SGD & 22% Net

Within the first 30 days of executing our conversion engineering protocol, the impact on consumer psychology and purchasing behavior became undeniable. Driven by our dedicated management team, a completely unknown brand generated $23,000 SGD in first-month revenue.

More importantly, our FBM infrastructure secured a highly lucrative 22% net profit margin, delivering nearly $5,000 SGD in pure profit straight out of the gate. However, early revenue spikes are merely proof of concept; our primary objective remained fixed on scaling sustainable sales volume and compounding long-term brand equity.
